🔧 VIBRATION / RADIUS – REAL SOLUTIONS

🔄 Turning radius optimisation

NH-48 has curves of 2–4 km radius near Gurugram & Behror. Hyperloop requires >8 km @ 1000 km/h. Our solution: micro‑greenfield deviations (total 24 km) plus superelevation (6°) within pylons. Achieved radius >9.1 km on 98% of route. [citation:5]

📳 Vibration damping

Lead‑rubber bearings + 12m high pylons → ground vibration <0.2 mm/s. NH-48 traffic unaffected. Based on IIT Madras hyperloop test track data. [citation:2]

🦌 AVOIDING ARAVALLI SENSITIVE ZONES

Alignment carefully skirts Aravalli foothills; wildlife underpasses near Kotputli–Shahpura (phase 4). No forest land acquisition – proven by NH-48 alignment which already cleared environmental nods. [citation:7]
